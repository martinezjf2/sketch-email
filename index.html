We’ve just released our latest Sketch update, which introduces Smart Animate — a new way to bring movement to your prototypes with smooth, animated transitions. Along with it, we’ve added support for Color Variables in gradients, a quick way to replace Libraries, and lots more!
Over on the web app, we’ve made it easier for developers to export any layer from the browser and given the UI a further refresh — there’s a new font, a cleaner look, and redesigned thumbnails.
